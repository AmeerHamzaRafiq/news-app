About Us

Tesla, Inc. is a prominent and innovative company known for its leadership in the electric vehicle (EV) industry and its efforts to promote sustainable energy solutions. Founded in 2003, Tesla has become a major player in the automotive world, producing a range of electric vehicles, including the Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y, which are known for their performance, cutting-edge technology, and long electric driving ranges.
